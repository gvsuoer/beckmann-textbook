Exercise 4.

There are free apps online to help you make sense of combining like terms and adding polynomials using algebra tiles. Use the apps on this website 8 . Read the introductory pages carefully. These pages tell you how to use the apps. Navigate through the introduction using the arrow in the upper right corner. Watch the videos and play with the apps for Simplifying Expressions and Adding Polynomials. Solve at least 2 problems at each difficulty level, until you conceptually understand the link between the algebra tiles and the expressions they represent. This work should take at most 20 minutes.
