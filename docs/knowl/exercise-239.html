Exercise 5.

Work on Linear Function Card Sort. You may discuss your work with other members of your group. You will be submitting your individual work as well as group work. Create and print graphs electronically (Desmos is recommended). If your handwriting is hard to read, type your responses.

Due in class on:

On the due date, each group will collect:

  1. The individual work completed by group members,

  2. Individual reflections on group functioning (Exercise 2.7.6), and

  3. The following group reflection, Reflecting on Your Group's Work on the Linear Function Card Sort.

Place the group reflection on top of the packet (Item 2.7.5.c), attach the individual work to it (Item 2.7.5.a and Item 2.7.5.b), then the group's work on the Linear Function Card Sort.
