There are free apps online to help you make sense of multiplying factors and factoring quadratic expressions using algebra tiles. Use the app on media.mivu.org 40 . Read the introductory pages carefully. These pages tell you how to use the apps. Navigate through the introduction using the arrow in the upper right corner. Watch the videos and play with the apps for Multiplication Activities. Look for Multiplication and Factoring Trinomials. Solve at least 2 problems at each difficulty level, until you conceptually understand the link between the algebra tiles and the expressions they represent.
